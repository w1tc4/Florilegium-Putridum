## Satis Latinis Verbis // Pretty Latin words
| Word | Language | Literal Meaning | Connotation / Vibe |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Florilegium** | Latin | A gathering of flowers | An elegant collection of literary extracts. |
| **Putridum** | Latin | Rotten / Decayed | Physical decomposition or moral corruption. |
| **Obscurum** | Latin | Dark / Hidden | Shadowy, mysterious, or forbidden knowledge. |
| **Spiritus Vitalis** | Latin | "The Vital Spirit" | The historical medical belief that blood carried the breath of life.
